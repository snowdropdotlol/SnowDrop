# SnowDrop: The Mechanics of Clean Distribution

Capital demands velocity. Tokens demand dispersion. The current model of token distribution fractures under its own friction. Deployers must secure capital to fund network fees. Networks must endure brute-force airdrops directed at dormant or parasitic addresses. SnowDrop reconstructs the supply pipeline, eliminating the cost of distribution while aggressively enforcing demographic quality.

This is not a dashboard. It is a liquidation and routing engine designed for surgical equity structure.

## The Friction of Genesis

The lifecycle of a decentralized asset begins with concentration. A single entity controls the supply. To achieve market legitimacy, the entity must disperse the asset. In traditional architectures, this incurs a strict capital tax. Dispersing a token to thousands of wallets requires the deployer to purchase transaction gas across separate intervals. This barrier breeds hesitation, stalls momentum, and encourages exploitative setups where developers abandon assets rather than spend liquid capital to distribute them.

We erase the tax. 

## The Melt

SnowDrop introduces an autonomous funding layer called The Melt.

A deployer deposits a designated portion of the asset supply into the protocol. The protocol immediately excises a rigid two-and-a-half percent of the deposit. It routes this slice through an aggregator to secure immediate liquidity in the native network coin. The deployer spends nothing. The protocol funds its own operations.

This converts dormant supply into operational fuel. Friction drops to zero.

## Demographic Selection

Brute-force distribution destroys value. Sending assets to bot clusters, sybil rings, or inactive accounts dilutes the capitalization table. The protocol actively prevents value destruction through an integrated intelligence layer.

The system evaluates addresses across three primary vectors:
1. **Holding Duration:** The protocol measures endurance. Capital assigned to addresses lacking historical permanence is lost capital.
2. **Cluster Detection:** Algorithmic graphs identify synchronized transactional behaviors, severing automated farms from the distribution pipeline.
3. **Cross-Network Action:** The system rewards provable humanity, measuring diverse contract interactions over isolated, repetitive farming operations.

Addresses that survive this gauntlet receive a structured score. The highest scores receive capital. The remnants receive nothing.

## Deflation and Yield

An infrastructure protocol must capture value. We mandate a sustainable structural return.

The revenue generated by the system undergoes a binary split. A portion is redirected to holders of the protocol's native token as direct network yield. The equivalent portion triggers consecutive market buybacks, intentionally removing supply from circulation. Volume generates deflation. Participation guarantees yield.

The outcome is an aggressive economic engine that permanently cleans the ecosystem while enriching its active participants.
